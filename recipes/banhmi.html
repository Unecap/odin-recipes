Bánh Mì

Recipes for Banh mi

Description

An affordable Vietnamese sandwich stuffed with pork and veggies

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Eggs
  • Pork
  • Cucumbers
  • Mayonnaise
  • Chili Sauce

Instruction

  1. Chop the cucumber into slices
  2. Wash the cilantro
  3. Cook the egg into omelettes
  4. Marinate and grill the pork
  5. Create a cut in the baguette. Make sure not to split it in half
  6. Spread the sauce into the baguette
  7. Stuff the pork -> eggs -> cucumbers -> cilantro into the baguette
  8. Enjoy
