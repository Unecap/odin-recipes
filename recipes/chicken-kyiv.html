Chicken Kyiv

Recipes for Chicken Kyiv

Description

A chicken fillet filled with juicy butter

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Butter
  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Eggs

Instruction

  1. Mix the butter with parsley and salt, then put into the fridge until it's firm
  2. Cut the butter into rectangular prism
  3. Create a hole in the chicken breast
  4. Stuff the butter into the hole, then cover it
  5. Coat the chicken breast into flour -> eggs -> panko
  6. Fry the chicken breast until it's golden, then baked it for 12 minutes
  7. Enjoy
