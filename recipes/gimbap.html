Gimbap

Recipes for gimbap

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Gim
  • Carrots
  • Hot Dogs
  • Eggs

Instruction

  1. Fry the eggs
  2. Chop the carrots, eggs, and hot dogs into long strips
  3. Set the gim on a bamboo mat
  4. Place the rice on the gim, then place the carrots, eggs, and hot dog strips
  5. Roll the gimbap
  6. Enjoy
